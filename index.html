Rambonomics

LamboRambo is a deflationary cryptocurrency. Every time a buy takes place with LamboRambo 4% of that transaction is sent to the liquidity pool and removed from the total supply. Every time a token is sold, 2% will be sent to the dead coin wallet increasing the scarcity of every LamboRambo token in circulation. What this means is that every time LamboRambo is used, the value of the remaining LamboRambo tokens in circulation is increased.

37% burned on release and sento the dead coin wallet
4% buy tax sent to the liquidity pool. this will help to keep the bots away and increase price stability while also ensuring that if you wanr to sell, there will be sufficient funds to do do
2% sell tax which will be burned, this will help increase the value of the token

no presale, this will ensure a level playing field and make sure that the price doesn't spike and the immediately drop on the first day
some tokens like to advertise that they have anti-whale mechanisms, but I can assure you that no such mechanism really exists. Anybody can easily generate thousands of wallets in a second, so we are not imposing any buy or sell limits
Liquidity locked in Pancake swap for two years
Regular airdrops for all RamboLambo hodlers

The funds in the dev wallet will eventually be cashed in to fund RamboDEX, an autonomous decentralised exchange that will be built on Thorchain. What's special about Thorchain is that you can directly swap any cryptocurrency for any other cryptocurrency without first having to purchase for example BNB or Ethereum. Anybody hodling LamboRambo will be able to swap their tokens for RamboDEX tokens at a 1:1 ratio upon launch. In addition we are building a NFT marketplace for musical artists.
